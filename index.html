Frequently asked questions

Future is rolling out city by city so every new member gets a matchmaker who actually knows their city and can find good matches nearby. The waitlist lets us bring people in gradually as we open up each city, instead of launching everywhere at once and stretching the experience thin.

It depends on your position and how fast your city fills up. Signing up early helps, and sharing your invite link moves you up the list faster since referred friends jump you ahead. We'll email you the moment your spot opens up.

Once you're off the waitlist, your first conversation with the matchmaker usually takes about 10-15 minutes. It asks about who you are, what you're looking for, and your dating preferences so it can start finding better matches for you right away.

Future Dates is our done-for-you date planning feature. Once two people match and both opt in, each side puts in a small amount toward the date, and Future takes care of picking and booking a nearby cafe or activity that suits you both — so instead of endless back-and-forth over "where should we go," you just show up.

Legal

TL;DR — we only collect what we need to match you well, we don't sell your info, and you can cancel or delete your account anytime. Be kind, be safe, and we'll handle the rest.

Privacy Policy

We collect the information you give us when you join the waitlist and use Future — things like your basic details, preferences, and conversations with the matchmaker — solely to find you better matches and improve the experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we only share it with the people and services needed to run the app (like payment processing for Future Dates or basic infrastructure providers), under confidentiality obligations. You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@heyfuture.site.

Terms of Service

By joining the waitlist or using Future, you agree to use the app respectfully and honestly — accurate profile information, no harassment, no impersonation, and no using the platform to harm or deceive other members. Future Dates bookings are subject to availability and the policies of the venues involved. We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate these terms. We may update these terms as the product evolves, and we'll let you know when we do.
